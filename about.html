Our Mission & Program

Our student-led outreach program at St. Maria de Matthias creates joyful experiences for children through carefully planned activities and community building.

Key Objectives

  • Create a welcoming environment where every child feels valued
  • Organize interactive games that promote teamwork
  • Provide nutritious meals and celebration treats
  • Develop leadership skills in our student volunteers
  • Build lasting memories for all participants

Program Details

Over four weeks, our team of 9-St. Paul Marist students works with school staff to:

  • Plan age-appropriate activities
  • Prepare healthy meals
  • Coordinate safety measures
  • Evaluate program impact
